About Carbon Footprint

Understanding and reducing our carbon footprint for a sustainable future.

What is a Carbon Footprint?

Carbon footprint refers to the total amount of greenhouse gases produced by human activities, typically measured in equivalent tons of CO₂.

Why is it Important?

Reducing carbon footprints helps combat climate change and contributes to a healthier environment for future generations.

How Can You Reduce It?

  • Use public transport or cycle instead of driving.
  • Switch to renewable energy sources.
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle materials.
  • Conserve energy and water at home.
